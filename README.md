Emergency Dashboard - README
Overview
This repository contains a Power BI dashboard designed for visualizing and analyzing various metrics and KPIs related to patient visits, satisfaction, and demographics in a healthcare setting. The dashboard is tailored to provide insights into patient flow, wait times, satisfaction levels, and demographic breakdowns.

The dashboard allows healthcare administrators and analysts to quickly assess performance and identify areas for improvement within the emergency department or clinic.

Features
1. Total Patients Overview
Total Patients: Displays the total number of patients (9216) served.
Appointment Type: Breaks down the percentage of Administrative (50.04%) vs Clinical (49.96%) appointments.
2. Satisfaction and Wait Time
Average Satisfaction: Shows the average satisfaction score (5.47).
Service Not Rated: Percentage of patients who did not rate the service (75.10%).
Average Wait Time: Displays the average wait time for patients (35.26 minutes).
3. Patient Types
Walk-In Patients: Percentage of patients who were walk-ins (58.59%).
Referred Patients: Percentage of patients who were referred (41.41%).
4. Patient Visits Analysis
Patients by Weektype: Number of patient visits during weekdays (6.6K) vs weekends (2.6K).
Total Patients by Age Group: Displays the distribution of patients across different age groups, with adults being the majority.
Total Patients Visits by Year: Shows the trend of patient visits over the years 2019 (4338 visits) and 2020 (4878 visits).
Total Patients Visits by Month: Line graph showing patient visits across each month, with a peak in August (1024 visits).
5. Department Referrals
Total Patients by Department/Referral: Breakdown of patient referrals by department, with the highest being 'None' (5.4K) and notable departments like General Practice (1.8K) and Orthopedics (1.0K).
6. Demographic Analysis
Patient Race: Heatmap displaying the distribution of patients by race and age group. The darkest green indicates low wait time, providing insight into demographic trends.
7. Gender Distribution
Gender Breakdown: A simple visualization of gender distribution with Female (48.7%), Male (51.1%), and other categories (0.3%).
Usage
To use this dashboard:

Clone this repository to your local machine.
Open the Power BI file included in the repository.
The data source used for this dashboard should be connected to your organization's database, or you can replace it with a sample dataset.
Use the filters and selection options on the dashboard to interact with the visualizations and gain insights.
Dependencies
Power BI Desktop: Ensure you have the latest version installed.
Data Source: Connect your database or use a provided sample dataset.
